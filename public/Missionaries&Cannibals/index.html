How to Play

  • Move all missionaries and cannibals to the right bank.
  • The boat can carry only 2 people at a time.
  • Click a person to move them into the boat.
  • Click "Move Boat" to cross the river.
  • Click people inside the boat to unload them.
  • Never leave missionaries outnumbered by cannibals on either bank.
